Creative Entrepenuer, House Music DJ & Producer, Label Owner;
Sonya Alvarez shows shes a formidale force and with no sign of slowing down. Through years of crafting her skills behind the decks, she tests the crowd's limits physically, always giving 110% energy into every appearance. The range of her track selection has evolved with the ages and now spans from house, tech-house, techno, bass-house, and break-beats music to r&b, hip-hop, rap, and classics from any era. As a Puerto Rican born and raised in Detroit, Sonya knows hustle, & she knows grind. Sonya is traveling frequently to share her unique musical and styling flavors with crowds all over the US, but especially in her beloved hometown, the 313. Her passion and drive is infused into everything she does, including her fashion styling. You can snag some of her merch as well as some of her closet pieces in her online store. You've only seen the beginning of Sonya Alvarez!
