Detroit-raised DJ/Producer Sonya Alvarez is Ready for Crossfade Combat. The tech house temptress has consistently rattled cabinets and posteriors for the past decade in both her own headlined appearances and as direct support for high caliber acts. Between a continued string of releases, operating as leader & founder of Korner Kombo Records, and launching a new live stream series featuring rotating cosplays, Sonya is moving in for the kill with no signs of relenting.
